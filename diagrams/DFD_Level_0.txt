                     └──────────────────────┘

## Description

**Level 0** shows the entire FEPD system as a single process with its external entities:

- **Actor**: Forensic Analyst (investigator who operates the tool)
- **Input**: Forensic disk images (E01, RAW, DD formats)
- **Output**: Timeline visualizations and PDF reports

The system operates as a black box at this level, with no internal decomposition.
